tour program

First day (Tuesday, March 29)

first day

Chabahar port is a small city, but the surrounding cities are far from the center of Chabahar. For this reason, when traveling to Chabahar, you need to travel long distances to the east, west and north of Chabahar. On the first day of the trip to Chabahar, we will start from the city itself and the port of Chabahar to get to know the traditional and social fabric of the city. You can count on Chabahar hotels to stay in this city because they have acceptable quality.

The second day (Wednesday, April 1)

second day

If we go from the center of Chabahar city and from near Chabahar Bay to the south and the coast, we will reach Darya Bozor Beach Park, one of the most famous parks and beach resorts of Chabahar. Bostan or the beach resort of the great sea has a unique and special view and a relaxing and beautiful environment. If you go a little east from the Great Sea Amusement Park, you will reach a rocky beach. This rocky beach has attracted a lot of attention due to its special location.

The third day (Thursday, April 2)

third day

On the third day of the trip, we move along the coastline and the first beach we come across is Remin Beach and Village. Remin Beach has a fishing pier with small and large fishing boats moored next to it. There is also a fish market nearby where you can buy freshly caught fish. But it is better to do your shopping on the way back so that the fish do not spoil along the way. Another thing about Remin is that some consider it the only beach in Iran with the ability to surf.

The fourth day (Friday, April 3)

The fourth day

On the fourth day, visiting Golfshan and Teng Wharf and understanding where palm trees, desert and sea meet, having lunch in one of Teng village houses with fresh sea fish with rice is part of the fourth day's plans. Visiting the green velvet beaches of Chabahar and Kanarak seas, the traditional market and the Chabahar Free Zone, having lunch at the Tehrani Chabahar restaurant, the best restaurant in Chabahar, is also included in the program of the fourth day. I hope you enjoy this trip.
